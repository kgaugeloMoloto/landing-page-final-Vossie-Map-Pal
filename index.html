Campus 

 The app will direct from the H-building which is the first building you see when you enter campus till the last building which is canteen , where food are sold.

Students 

 This app is to help students from Eduvos midrand to navigate to their classes and their favourite venues during break time.

The app  

 Currently the app provides with 4 places. which is the Canteen where students eat, the lab for bio med students, the It building whereyou can find computers and also H-building where you can get the reception. 

Chats 

Students can chat with their lectures and their class mates. They will get notified when there is a emergency and also announcemens.
